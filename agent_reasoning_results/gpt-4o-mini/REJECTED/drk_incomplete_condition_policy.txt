## Alternative Policy
"If you wish to reproduce images from the Digital Cultural Heritage Collection, please submit a request that specifies the type of image (e.g., photograph, artwork, manuscript) you would like to reproduce. Museum curators will review each request and may approve or deny it based on relevance and rights considerations. Requests that receive approval will be sent to the Rights Management Department for final processing. You will be notified regarding the outcome of your request."

## Improvement Suggestions
1. Clarify the definition of "images" to include specific types of materials available within the collection.
2. Introduce measurable constraints regarding the volume of requests to manage expectations.
3. Outline the process for denied requests to enhance transparency for requesters.
